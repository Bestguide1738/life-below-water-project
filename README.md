  The Life Below Water Project is an educational website dedicated to raising awareness about water pollution and its devastating effects on marine and freshwater species. The project highlights the causes of pollution, the impact on aquatic ecosystems, and practical solutions to restore and protect water bodies.

Key Focus Areas:
	•	Causes and types of water pollution
	•	Effects of pollution on aquatic life and biodiversity
	•	Real-life examples of affected species
	•	Preventive measures and restoration efforts
	•	How individuals and communities can take action

Goal:
To inform, inspire, and encourage people to make conscious choices that contribute to a cleaner, safer, and more sustainable aquatic environment for all living organisms.
